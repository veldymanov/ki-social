THE WORDPRESS HUBSPOT CONNECTOR

What is the WordPress HubSpot Connector?

The WordPress HubSpot Connector allows HubSpot users automatically to republish their content from WordPress. The Connector is installed as a WordPress plugin.

HubSpot Connector

  • Pushes your WordPress posts and RSS feeds to HubSpot
  • Implements HubSpot Tracking Code on your WordPress site

What can the WordPress HubSpot Connector do for you?

The Connector saves you time and automatically tracks audience engagement. It creates a solid integration between your WordPress site(s) and your HubSpot account. New posts and pages on your WordPress site will be published automatically through HubSpot. No more copy-pasting posts, no more missing content. New content will be published through HubSpot Social and tracked in Analytics.

Depending on your preference, you can set new WordPress posts or pages to be published automatically on HubSpot, or saved as drafts for further editing. WordPress HubSpot Connector allows existing HubSpot customers and trial users to install the HubSpot tracking code on their existing WordPress blogs and websites.
