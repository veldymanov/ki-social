TRAINING

Our training courses are carefully designed with practice in mind—we want to help you improve user adoption and productivity, reduce your reliance on external support, and maximize your investment to create a competitive edge. We offer online, remote with live video, or on site training. The following courses are available:

Learn how to measure and ensure your organization’s compliance with the new European data protection regulation. The course covers notification obligations, the mandatory data protection officer requirement, consent, cross-border data transfers, profiling, data portability, vendor management, anonymization, codes of conduct, certifications, penalties, and more.

Ensure best practices when it comes to privacy governance across your organization, including roles, overall framework, and risk management. We can help you to map out the consistent guidelines, procedures and business processes necessary for open data initiatives.

Learn the basics of de-identification requirements under HIPAA and Canadian privacy laws. Measure the maturity of your organization’s de-identification practices, and participate in practical tutorials for risk-based anonymization.

KI Design views privacy as an integrated process. This course teaches organizations how to incorporate privacy into their everyday operations to achieve blanket compliance and best practices.

We can enhance your company’s software development life cycle by implementing controls, technologies, and personal data handling best practices.
